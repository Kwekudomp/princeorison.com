## 1. Getting Started — Logging In

### How to access the admin portal

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Safari, or Edge)
2. Go to: **princeorison.com/admin**
3. Enter your email address: `princeorison1@gmail.com`
4. Enter your password
5. Click **Sign In**

You will be taken to your Dashboard.

### Signing out

Click your email address in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar, then click **Sign Out**.

> **Important:** Always sign out when using a shared or public computer.

## 2. Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your home page inside the admin portal. It shows you a quick summary of your site at a glance.

### What you can see on the Dashboard

| Section | What it tells you |
|---|---|
| **Published Products** | How many products are currently visible on your site |
| **New Arrivals** | How many products are currently marked as new arrivals |
| **Videos** | How many published videos you have |
| **Newsletter** | Total active email subscribers |

### Recent Enquiries

The bottom of the Dashboard shows the 5 most recent enquiries from customers. You can see their name, date, and current status.

### Navigation (Sidebar)

On the left side of the screen you will see the main menu:

- **Dashboard** — Home overview
- **Collections** — Manage your fashion categories
- **New Arrivals** — Announce new seasons and featured pieces
- **Videos** — Upload and manage video content
- **Enquiries** — Customer consultation requests
- **Newsletter** — Email subscribers

> On mobile, tap the **☰ menu icon** at the top to open the navigation.

## 3. Collections — Managing Your Categories

A **Collection** is a category on your website — for example, *KAFTAN (Casual)*, *AGBADA (3 Piece)*, or *GROOMSMAN/GROOMSWEAR*.

Go to: **Collections** in the sidebar.

---

### 3.1 Creating a New Collection

Use this when launching a brand new category — e.g. a *Harmattan Masterpiece* or *Eid Exclusive* line.

1. Click the **+ New Collection** button (top right)
2. Fill in the form:

| Field | What to enter | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Collection Name** | The display name shown on site | `Harmattan Collection 2025` |
| **URL Slug** | Auto-fills from the name. The web address. | `harmattan-collection-2025` |
| **Short Description** | One sentence shown on the grid card | `Premium pieces for the Harmattan season` |
| **Hero Description** | Longer paragraph shown at the top of the collection page | `Discover our curated Harmattan line...` |
| **Bespoke toggle** | Tick this if the collection is made-to-order only (no product photos — shows an "Enquire to Order" card instead) | ☑ for custom/bespoke lines |

3. Click **Create Collection**

Your new collection appears in the list and is immediately published on the website.

> **Tip:** After creating, click **Products →** to start adding items to it.

---

### 3.2 Editing a Collection

1. Find the collection in the list
2. Click **Edit**
3. Update the name, short description, or hero description
4. Click **Save Changes**

Changes appear on the website immediately.

---

### 3.3 Hiding / Showing a Collection

- Click **Hide** to remove a collection from the public website (it stays in your admin, nothing is deleted)
- Click **Publish** to make it visible again

Use this to temporarily take down a collection during a refresh, or to prepare a new one before it goes live.

---

### 3.4 Deleting a Collection

1. Click **Delete** on the collection row
2. Confirm the deletion

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Deleting a collection also permanently deletes all products inside it. This cannot be undone. Use **Hide** instead if you are not sure.

## 4. Products — Managing Items in a Collection

Click **Products →** on any collection to open the product editor for that collection.

---

### 4.1 Adding a New Product

1. Scroll to the bottom and click **+ Add New Product**
2. Fill in the form:

| Field | What to enter | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Name** | Product name | `Classic White Agbada` |
| **Description** | What the item is | `Crisp white 3-piece agbada with hand-embroidered trim` |
| **WhatsApp Message** | Pre-filled text when a customer taps "Order on WhatsApp" | `Hi, I'm interested in the Classic White Agbada` |
| **Features** | Bullet points (one per line) | `Premium cotton fabric` / `Complete 3-piece set` |

3. Click **Add Product**

---

### 4.2 Uploading Product Images

After adding a product (or when editing an existing one):

1. Click **Edit** on the product
2. In the **Product Images** section, click the **+ Add** tile
3. Select a photo from your device (JPEG, PNG, or WebP — max 5MB)
4. The image uploads and appears immediately

**To set the cover image** (the main photo shown in the grid):
- Hover over any image and click **Cover**
- A gold "Cover" badge will appear on it

**To delete an image:**
- Hover over the image and click **Del**

> **Tip:** Upload your best photo first — it becomes the cover automatically.

---

### 4.3 Editing a Product

1. Click **Edit** on the product
2. Update any fields — name, description, WhatsApp message, features
3. Click **Save Product**

---

### 4.4 Hiding / Showing a Product

- Click **Hide** to remove a product from the website (without deleting it)
- Click **Show** to make it visible again

---

### 4.5 Deleting a Product

Click **Delete** and confirm.

> ⚠️ This permanently deletes the product and all its images. Use **Hide** if unsure.

---

### 4.6 Uploading a Collection Cover Image

The cover image is the photo shown on the homepage collections grid for that category.

1. Open the collection editor (click **Products →**)
2. At the top, find **Collection Cover Image**
3. Click **Upload Cover**
4. Select your image

The homepage will update with the new cover image immediately.

## 5. New Arrivals — Season Announcements

This is where you announce a new season or featured drop to the entire homepage.

Go to: **New Arrivals** in the sidebar.

---

### 5.1 Setting the Season Title

The **Season Announcement** card controls the headline shown in the New Arrivals section on the homepage.

1. Update the **Section Title** — e.g. `Harmattan Collection 2025` or `Eid Exclusive Drop`
2. Update the **Subtitle / Tagline** — e.g. `Crafted for the season's finest occasions`
3. Check the **Preview** (dark box) to see exactly how it will appear
4. Click **Save & Publish**

The homepage updates immediately.

> **Examples of good season titles:**
> - `Harmattan Collection 2025`
> - `Eid Mubarak Collection`
> - `Valentine Special`
> - `New Season Arrivals`
> - `Wedding Season 2025`

---

### 5.2 Choosing Which Products Are Featured

The **All Products** table shows every published product across all collections.

**To add a product to New Arrivals:**
1. Find the product in the table
2. Toggle the switch on the right to **ON** (turns gold)
3. Choose a label: **New**, **Featured**, **Season**, or **Limited**

**To remove a product:**
1. Toggle the switch to **OFF**

**To filter by collection:**
Use the dropdown at the top right of the table to show only one collection's products.

**To start fresh (e.g. new season):**
Click **Clear all** in the "Currently Featured" section to remove all products at once, then re-select the new season's pieces.

---

### 5.3 Label Guide

| Label | When to use |
|---|---|
| **New** | Fresh piece, just added |
| **Featured** | Highlight / editor's pick |
| **Season** | Specific to the current season |
| **Limited** | Limited quantity or exclusive run |

## 6. Videos & Reels

Upload showcase videos, lookbooks, and behind-the-scenes reels. One video can be set as the **featured reel** that plays on the homepage.

Go to: **Videos** in the sidebar.

---

### 6.1 Uploading a Video

1. Click **+ Upload Video**
2. Click **Choose Video** and select your file
   - Accepted formats: **MP4, WebM, MOV**
   - Maximum size: **100MB**
   - Wait for "Video ready." to appear before continuing
3. (Optional) Click **Choose Thumbnail** to upload a poster image shown before the video plays
4. Fill in the details:

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| **Title** | Short name for the video |
| **Category** | Showcase, Lookbook, Behind the Scenes, or Reel |
| **Caption** | Optional short description |

5. Click **Save Video**

> **Tip:** The first video you upload is automatically set as the Homepage Featured reel.

---

### 6.2 Setting the Homepage Featured Video

Only one video plays on the homepage at a time.

1. Find the video you want to feature in the list
2. Click **Set Featured**

A gold **"Homepage Featured"** badge will appear on it.

---

### 6.3 Hiding / Deleting a Video

- Click **Hide** to remove it from public view (the file is kept)
- Click **Delete** to permanently remove it

## 7. Enquiries — Customer Messages

When a customer fills in the contact form on your website, their message appears here.

Go to: **Enquiries** in the sidebar.

---

### 7.1 Viewing Enquiries

Enquiries are shown newest first. Click on any row to expand it and see the full details:

- Customer name, phone, email
- Event type (wedding, casual, formal, etc.)
- Event date
- Their message
- Collection they are interested in

---

### 7.2 Replying to an Enquiry

**Via WhatsApp (recommended):**
1. Expand the enquiry
2. Click **Reply on WhatsApp**
3. WhatsApp opens with the customer's number and a pre-filled greeting — just add your reply

**Via Email:**
1. Expand the enquiry
2. Click **Reply by Email**
3. Your email app opens pre-addressed to the customer

> **Note:** When you click Reply on WhatsApp or Reply by Email, the status automatically changes to "Replied".

---

### 7.3 Managing Status

Use the **Status** dropdown inside an expanded enquiry to track where each conversation is:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **New** | Just received — needs your attention |
| **Read** | You have seen it but not replied yet |
| **Replied** | You have responded to the customer |
| **Closed** | Conversation complete / no further action needed |

The red badge on the **Enquiries** sidebar item shows how many are still **New**.

## 8. Newsletter — Subscribers

This page shows everyone who has signed up to receive updates from Prince Orison.

Go to: **Newsletter** in the sidebar.

---

### 8.1 Viewing Subscribers

The list shows each subscriber's email, where they signed up (source), and the date they joined.

**To search:** Type in the search box at the top to filter by email address.

---

### 8.2 Copying Emails

Click **Copy Emails** to copy all active subscribers' email addresses to your clipboard.

You can then paste them directly into:
- **Gmail** BCC field
- **Mailchimp** or any other email marketing tool
- A spreadsheet

---

### 8.3 Deactivating / Reactivating a Subscriber

- Click **Deactivate** to unsubscribe someone (they remain in the list but are excluded from the copy)
- Click **Reactivate** to re-enable them

## Tips & Best Practices

**Images**
- Use portrait photos (taller than wide) for product images — they display best in the cards
- Minimum recommended size: 800 × 1000 pixels
- Keep file sizes under 3MB for fast loading

**Collections**
- Hide collections during a photoshoot or refresh rather than deleting them
- Always add a cover image — collections without one show a dark placeholder

**New Arrivals**
- Update the season title each time you launch new pieces — it makes the homepage feel fresh
- Aim for 3–6 products in the New Arrivals section for the best visual layout
- Use the **Clear all** button at the start of each new season before re-selecting

**Videos**
- Film in good lighting — even a phone video looks professional with the right light
- Vertical (portrait) videos work best for reels
- Keep showcase videos under 2 minutes

**Enquiries**
- Check the admin portal at least once a day so no enquiry goes unanswered
- Mark enquiries as **Closed** once resolved — this keeps your inbox clean and the badge count accurate
