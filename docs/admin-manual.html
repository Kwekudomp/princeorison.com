Getting Started

How to log in and navigate the admin portal

How to Log In
  1. 1
    Open your web browser Use Google Chrome, Safari, or Microsoft Edge for the best experience
  2. 2
    Go to the admin portal Type in the address bar: princeorison.com/admin
  3. 3
    Enter your email address princeorison1@gmail.com
  4. 4
    Enter your password and click Sign In You will be taken directly to your Dashboard

Sign out when done. Always click Sign Out (bottom of the sidebar) when finished, especially on a shared phone or computer. Your session stays active until you sign out.

Collections

Create and manage your fashion categories

Creating a New Collection

Use this when launching a brand new category — e.g. a Harmattan Masterpiece or Eid Exclusive line.

  1. 1
    Go to Collections in the sidebarThen click the gold "+ New Collection" button at the top right
  2. 2
    Fill in the collection detailsName, URL slug (auto-fills), short description, and hero description
  3. 3
    Tick "Bespoke" if it's made-to-order onlyBespoke collections show an "Enquire to Order" card with no product images
  4. 4
    Click Create CollectionThe collection is immediately published. Click "Products →" to add items.
Collection Fields Explained
FieldWhat to enterExample
Collection NameThe display name shown on siteHarmattan Collection 2025
URL SlugAuto-fills from the name. The web address path.harmattan-collection-2025
Short DescriptionOne sentence shown on the homepage cardPremium pieces for the Harmattan season
Hero DescriptionA longer paragraph shown at the top of the collection's own pageDiscover our curated Harmattan line…
Bespoke toggleTick this for made-to-order collections with no product photos✓ for custom / political suit lines

Hide a collection to temporarily remove it from the website without deleting anything. Click Publish to bring it back.

Delete is permanent. Deleting a collection also deletes all products inside it. Use Hide instead if you are not sure.

Videos & Reels

Upload showcase videos and set your homepage featured reel

Uploading a Video
  1. 1
    Click "+ Upload Video"
  2. 2
    Choose your video fileMP4, WebM, or MOV — max 100MB
  3. 3
    Wait for "Video ready."Do not close the page during upload
  4. 4
    Add a title and category
  5. 5
    Click Save Video
Featured Homepage Reel

Only one video shows on the homepage at a time.

To change it: find your video in the list → click "Set Featured". A gold Homepage Featured badge appears on it.

Your first uploaded video is automatically set as featured.

Enquiries

Customer consultation requests from the website

How to Reply to an Enquiry
  1. 1
    Go to Enquiries in the sidebarNew messages are shown with an amber "new" badge
  2. 2
    Click on the customer's row to expand itSee their name, phone, email, event type, date, and message
  3. 3
    Click "Reply on WhatsApp" (recommended)WhatsApp opens with their number and a pre-filled greeting. Add your reply and send.
  4. 4
    The status automatically changes to "Replied"Or manually update the status dropdown to keep track
Enquiry Status Guide
New
Just received — needs your attention today
Read
You have seen it but not replied yet
Replied
You have responded to the customer
Closed
Conversation complete — no further action needed

Check enquiries every day. The red badge on the sidebar shows how many new messages are waiting. Mark enquiries as Closed once resolved to keep the count accurate.

Newsletter Subscribers

View and export your email list

Exporting Emails
  1. 1
    Go to Newsletter
  2. 2
    Click "Copy Emails"All active subscriber emails are copied
  3. 3
    Paste into your email toolGmail BCC, Mailchimp, or a spreadsheet
Finding a Subscriber

Use the search box at the top of the Newsletter page to filter by email address.

To deactivate someone (e.g. they asked to unsubscribe), click Deactivate next to their email. They stay in the list but are excluded from the copied list.

Best Practices

Photos
  • Use portrait photos — taller than wide
  • Minimum 800 × 1000 pixels
  • Keep files under 3MB for fast loading
  • Upload your best photo first — it becomes the cover
New Arrivals
  • Update season title for every new drop
  • Keep 3 – 6 products featured at a time
  • Use Clear All at the start of each season
  • Use Limited label to create urgency
Enquiries
  • Check the portal at least once daily
  • Reply via WhatsApp for fastest response
  • Mark as Closed when done to keep count clean
  • Filter by "new" to see only urgent messages
Videos
  • Film in good natural lighting
  • Portrait (vertical) videos work best
  • Keep reels under 2 minutes
  • MP4 format gives best compatibility
Collections
  • Hide instead of deleting during refresh
  • Always add a cover image to collections
  • Write clear hero descriptions — customers read them
  • Keep descriptions between 2 – 4 sentences
Newsletter
  • Copy emails before every campaign
  • Send campaigns on Fridays or Sundays
  • Always BCC — never CC — for privacy
  • Announce new seasons to subscribers first
